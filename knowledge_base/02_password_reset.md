# Password Reset

Forgot your password? Follow these steps to reset it quickly and securely.

## How to Reset Your Password

1. Go to the login page at `https://app.example.com/login`
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"** below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
5. Check your email for a password reset link (valid for 1 hour)
6. Click the link and enter your new password
7. Confirm the new password and click **"Reset Password"**

## Password Requirements

Your new password must:
- Be at least **8 characters** long
- Contain at least **1 uppercase letter**
- Contain at least **1 lowercase letter**
- Contain at least **1 number**
- Contain at least **1 special character** (e.g., `!@#$%^&*`)

## Didn't Receive the Email?

- Check your **spam or junk folder**
- Wait up to **5 minutes** — emails can be delayed
- Make sure you're using the **correct email address**
- Try the **Resend** option on the reset page

## Password Reset Link Expired

Reset links expire after **1 hour** for security. Simply request a new one from the forgot password page.

## Still Locked Out?

If you've lost access to your email or cannot reset, contact support:
- Email: support@example.com
- Live Chat: Available 9am–6pm UTC, Monday–Friday
