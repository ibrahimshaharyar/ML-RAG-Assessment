# Team Management

Collaborate with your team by inviting members, assigning roles, and managing access.

## Inviting Team Members

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **"Invite Member"**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role (see below)
5. Click **"Send Invite"**

The invitee receives an email with a link valid for **48 hours**. If it expires, resend from the same page.

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|------------|
| **Owner** | Full access including billing, can delete the account |
| **Admin** | Manage team, API keys, settings; cannot manage billing |
| **Member** | Submit queries, view own query history |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to shared dashboards |

> Only one **Owner** at a time. To transfer ownership: Settings → Team → Transfer Ownership.

## Removing a Team Member

1. Settings → Team
2. Find the member and click the three dots (⋯)
3. Select **"Remove from Team"**
4. Their access is revoked immediately
5. Any API keys they created remain active — rotate them manually

## Pending Invitations

View and manage pending invitations in **Settings → Team → Pending Invites**.
You can resend or revoke an invite at any time.

## Member Limits by Plan

| Plan | Max Team Members |
|------|-----------------|
| Free | 1 (owner only) |
| Starter | 5 |
| Pro | 25 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

## Team Activity

Owners and Admins can see team activity in **Settings → Audit Logs**, including:
- Who logged in and when
- Queries submitted by each member
- Changes to settings or API keys
