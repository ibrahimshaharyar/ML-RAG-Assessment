All paid plans receive a **20% discount** when billed annually:
- Starter Annual: **$182/year** (saves $46)
- Pro Annual: **$758/year** (saves $190)

## Changing Your Plan

**To upgrade:**
1. Settings → Billing → Change Plan
2. Select your new plan
3. Confirm — you're charged the prorated difference immediately

**To downgrade:**
1. Settings → Billing → Change Plan
2. Select the lower plan
3. Downgrade takes effect at your next billing date — you keep current features until then

## Free Plan Limits

- 100 API calls per day (resets at midnight UTC)
- 1 workspace only
- No team collaboration features
- Community support only (no email/chat)
