# Invoice Download

You can download invoices for all past payments from your billing dashboard.

## How to Download an Invoice

1. Log in and go to **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**
2. Find the invoice by date or amount
3. Click the **Download PDF** button next to it
4. The invoice PDF will be saved to your device

## Invoice Information Included

Each invoice contains:
- Invoice number and date
- Your company name and billing address
- Itemised list of charges
- Tax breakdown (if applicable)
- Payment method used (last 4 digits)
- Total amount paid

## Bulk Invoice Download

To download multiple invoices at once:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**
2. Click **"Export All"** to download a ZIP file of all invoice PDFs
3. Or filter by date range first, then export

## Updating Billing Information on Invoices

To add your company name, VAT number, or address to future invoices:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing Details**
2. Update the fields and click **Save**
3. This applies to **future invoices only** — past invoices cannot be modified

## Missing Invoice?

- Invoices are generated within **1 hour** of a successful payment
- Check your registered email for an automatic invoice email
- If an invoice is missing after 24 hours, contact billing@example.com with your payment date and amount

## Tax & VAT Invoices

If you require a VAT invoice:
- Add your VAT number in **Settings → Billing → Billing Details**
- VAT invoices are automatically generated for EU customers
