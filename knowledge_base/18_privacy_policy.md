# Privacy Policy Summary

This is a plain-language summary of our full privacy policy. Our goal is transparency.

## What We Collect

- **Account info**: Name, email, encrypted password
- **Usage data**: Queries sent, API calls, timestamps, latency
- **Payment info**: Processed securely by Stripe — we store only last 4 digits and billing address
- **Technical data**: IP address, browser type, device type (for security purposes)

## What We Do NOT Collect

- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not use your queries to train AI models without your explicit consent
- We do not collect sensitive personal info (race, religion, health data)

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide and improve our services
- To send transactional emails (account, billing, security alerts)
- To detect fraud and abuse
- To comply with legal obligations

## Who Sees Your Data

| Party | Access | Reason |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Our engineering team | Limited | Debugging only |
| Stripe | Billing data | Payment processing |
| AWS | All encrypted data | Infrastructure |
| Legal authorities | Required by law | Court orders only |

## Cookies

We use essential cookies only (session, CSRF protection). No tracking or advertising cookies.

## Marketing Emails

We only send marketing emails with your explicit opt-in. You can unsubscribe at any time from the email footer or in **Settings → Notifications**.
