## Before Contacting Support

Please have the following ready to speed up your case:
- Your **account email address**
- The **error message** (exact text)
- Your **request ID** (found in API error responses as `request_id`)
- Steps to **reproduce** the issue
- Your **plan** and approximate time the issue started

## Priority Support (Pro & Enterprise)

Pro and Enterprise customers receive priority queue placement:
- **Pro**: < 4 hour response via email
- **Enterprise**: Dedicated Slack channel + < 1 hour response

To access your priority support Slack, contact your account manager.

## Submitting a Bug Report

1. Email support@example.com with subject: **"Bug Report: [brief description]"**
2. Include:
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Expected vs actual behaviour
   - Screenshots or logs if available
3. We triage within 1 business day and keep you updated
