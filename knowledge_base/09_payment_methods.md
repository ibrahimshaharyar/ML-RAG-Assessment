# Payment Methods

We accept a variety of payment methods to make billing as flexible as possible.

## Accepted Payment Methods

| Method | Availability |
|--------|-------------|
| Visa / Mastercard / Amex | All countries |
| PayPal | All countries |
| Bank Transfer (ACH) | US only |
| SEPA Direct Debit | EU only |
| Google Pay | Select countries |

## Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
3. Select your method type and enter details
4. Click **"Save"** — we run a small verification charge (refunded immediately)

## Setting a Default Payment Method

If you have multiple methods saved:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click the three dots (⋯) next to the method
3. Select **"Set as Default"**

## Removing a Payment Method

You must have at least one payment method if you have an active paid subscription.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click the three dots (⋯) next to the method
3. Select **"Remove"**

## Payment Security

- All payment data is processed by **Stripe** — we never store raw card numbers
- Payments are encrypted with **TLS 1.3**
- We are **PCI DSS Level 1** compliant

## Failed Payments

If your payment fails:
- Verify your card has sufficient funds and hasn't expired
- Check with your bank for any blocks on the transaction
- Try adding a different card as your default
- Contact billing@example.com if the issue persists
