# Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time. Please read this guide carefully — **account deletion is irreversible**.

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account

- All your data, projects, and API keys are **permanently deleted**
- Your subscription is **immediately cancelled** (no refund for unused time)
- You will **lose access** to all services immediately
- Your email address can be re-used to create a new account after 30 days

## Before You Delete

We recommend:
- **Exporting your data** (see Data Export guide)
- **Cancelling any active subscriptions** to avoid future charges
- **Removing team members** if you manage a team account

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings → Account**
3. Scroll to the bottom — **Danger Zone** section
4. Click **"Delete My Account"**
5. Type your email address to confirm
6. Click **"Permanently Delete"**

Your account will be **scheduled for deletion within 24 hours**. During this window, you can cancel by logging back in.

## Team Account Deletion

If you are the **owner** of a team:
- You must **transfer ownership** or **remove all team members** before deleting
- Team data including shared projects will be permanently erased

## I Changed My Mind

If your account is within the **24-hour grace period**, log back in and you'll see an option to **Cancel Deletion**.

After the grace period, recovery is not possible.
