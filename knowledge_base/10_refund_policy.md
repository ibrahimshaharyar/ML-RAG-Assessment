# Refund Policy

We offer refunds in specific circumstances. Please read this policy before requesting a refund.

## Eligibility for Refunds

| Situation | Refund Eligible? |
|-----------|-----------------|
| First 7 days of a new subscription | ✅ Full refund |
| Accidental duplicate charge | ✅ Full refund |
| Service outage > 4 hours | ✅ Prorated credit |
| Plan downgrade mid-cycle | ❌ No refund (keeps features until end of cycle) |
| Annual plan cancellation after 7 days | ❌ No refund |
| API overuse charges | ❌ No refund |

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email billing@example.com with subject: **"Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"**
2. Include:
   - Your account email
   - Invoice number
   - Reason for refund request
3. We respond within **2 business days**
4. Approved refunds are processed within **5–10 business days** to your original payment method

## 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

New subscribers on Starter or Pro plans are eligible for a **full refund within 7 days** of their first payment — no questions asked.

To claim:
- Email billing@example.com within 7 days of your first charge
- Your account will be downgraded to Free after the refund is processed

## Service Credits

For service outages (>4 hours of downtime):
- Credits are applied to your account automatically
- Credits appear in **Settings → Billing → Credits**
- Credits are applied to your next invoice

## Annual Plan Refunds

Annual plans refunded within the first **7 days** receive a full refund. After 7 days, annual plans are **non-refundable**, but you may cancel to prevent future renewals.
