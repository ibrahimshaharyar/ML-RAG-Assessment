# Account Creation Guide

Welcome to our platform! This guide walks you through creating a new account.

## Steps to Create an Account

1. Visit our homepage at `https://app.example.com`
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top-right corner
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name**: Your legal or display name
   - **Email Address**: Must be a valid, active email
   - **Password**: At least 8 characters, one uppercase, one number
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your inbox for a verification email
6. Click the verification link within 24 hours

## Email Verification

If you did not receive the verification email:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Use the **Resend Verification Email** button on the login page
- Make sure the email address you entered is correct

## Troubleshooting

- **"Email already in use"**: Try logging in or use **Forgot Password**
- **Form won't submit**: Ensure all required fields are filled
- **Verification link expired**: Request a new one from the login page
