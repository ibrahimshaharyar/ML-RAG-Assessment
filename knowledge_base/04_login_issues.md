# Login Issues & Troubleshooting

Having trouble logging in? Here are the most common issues and how to fix them.

## Common Login Problems

### 1. Incorrect Email or Password
- Double-check for typos in your email
- Passwords are **case-sensitive**
- Try **"Forgot Password"** if unsure

### 2. Account Locked
After **5 consecutive failed login attempts**, your account is temporarily locked for 30 minutes.
- Wait 30 minutes and try again
- Or reset your password immediately to unlock

### 3. 2FA Code Not Working
- Ensure your phone's clock is synced (Settings → Date & Time → Set Automatically)
- The code refreshes every 30 seconds — wait for a fresh one
- Try a backup code if you have one

### 4. "Account Not Verified"
Your email address hasn't been verified yet.
- Check for the verification email
- Click **"Resend Verification"** on the login page

### 5. Browser Issues
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser or Incognito/Private mode
- Disable browser extensions that may block scripts

### 6. Single Sign-On (SSO) Errors
- Ensure your organization's SSO is configured correctly
- Contact your IT admin if you see "SSO configuration error"

## Still Can't Log In?

Contact support with:
- Your registered email address
- The exact error message you see
- What browser and OS you're using
