# Billing Overview

This guide explains how billing works, what you're charged for, and how to manage your billing settings.

## How Billing Works

- Subscriptions are billed **monthly or annually** depending on your plan
- Annual plans receive a **20% discount** compared to monthly billing
- Billing occurs on the **same day each month** (your billing date)
- You will receive an email invoice after every successful payment

## Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Log in and go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Your current plan, renewal date, and next charge amount are displayed

## Upgrading or Downgrading

- **Upgrade**: Takes effect immediately; you're charged a prorated amount
- **Downgrade**: Takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle

## Payment Failure

If a payment fails:
- We retry automatically after **3 days**, then again after **7 days**
- You will receive email notifications for each failed attempt
- After 3 failed attempts, your account is **downgraded to the Free plan**
- Update your payment method to reactivate your subscription

## Billing Cycle

| Event | When |
|-------|------|
| Initial charge | At sign-up |
| Renewal charge | Monthly on billing date |
| Prorated upgrade | Immediately on upgrade |
| Downgrade | End of current cycle |

## Taxes

Applicable taxes (VAT, GST, sales tax) are calculated based on your billing address and added to your invoice.
